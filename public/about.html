About Glancio

Transforming retail intelligence through AI-powered camera analytics

Our Mission

At Glancio, we believe that every retail business deserves access to intelligent insights that drive growth and efficiency. Our mission is to democratize retail analytics by making AI-powered camera intelligence accessible, affordable, and privacy-first.

We're transforming how retailers understand their customers, optimize their operations, and protect their assets through cutting-edge technology that respects privacy and delivers actionable insights.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Glancio emerged from a simple observation: traditional retail analytics were either too expensive, too complex, or too invasive. We saw an opportunity to create something different - a solution that combines the power of AI with a fundamental respect for privacy.

Our team of retail experts, AI specialists, and privacy advocates came together with a shared vision: to build retail intelligence that works for everyone, from small local stores to large retail chains.

Our Values

Privacy-First

We believe that powerful analytics and privacy protection can coexist. Our technology is built from the ground up with privacy at its core, ensuring that customer identities are protected while still delivering valuable insights.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of what's possible in retail analytics, developing new AI models and features that help our customers stay ahead of the competition.

Accessibility

We're committed to making advanced retail intelligence accessible to businesses of all sizes, not just the largest corporations.

Transparency

We believe in being open about how our technology works, what data we collect, and how we protect it. Trust is earned through transparency.

Our Technology

Glancio's platform combines advanced computer vision, machine learning, and edge computing to deliver real-time retail intelligence. Our technology includes:

  • AI-Powered Analytics: Advanced algorithms that understand customer behavior patterns
  • Privacy Protection: Automatic face blurring and data anonymization
  • Real-Time Processing: Instant insights and alerts
  • Scalable Architecture: Designed to grow with your business
  • ISO 27001 Certified: Enterprise-grade security standards

Our Impact

Since our launch, Glancio has helped retailers across various industries:

  • Increase Conversions: Average 23% improvement in conversion rates
  • Reduce Loss: 40% reduction in retail theft and shrinkage
  • Optimize Layouts: 15% improvement in product placement effectiveness
  • Enhance Customer Experience: Better service through real-time insights
