Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Glancio collects information to provide and improve our retail analytics services. We collect:

  • Business Information: Company name, contact details, industry, and business size
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform and analytics
  • Technical Data: Device information, IP addresses, and browser data
  • Camera Analytics: Behavioral patterns and retail insights (no personal identification)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our retail analytics services
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Protection & Privacy-First Approach

Faces Blurred by Default

At Glancio, we prioritize privacy. All faces in camera footage are automatically blurred before any data leaves your premises, ensuring no personal identification is possible.

No PII Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). Our analytics focus on behavioral patterns and retail insights only.

ISO 27001 Certified

Our security practices meet international standards, ensuring your data is protected with enterprise-grade security measures.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With trusted service providers who assist in our operations

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data and request copies
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Analytics data is typically retained for up to 2 years unless you request deletion.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure all international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and maintain the same level of protection.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@glancio.com

Address: Glancio Privacy Team
